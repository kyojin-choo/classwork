Roelof T. Bruintjes: Ph. D.

  • Affiliate Professor

    • Research Interests: Cloud physics, mesoscale modeling, and weather modifications

    Dr. Bruintjes works on the improvement of microphysical parameterization in numerical cloud- and mesoscale models, evaluating cloud seeding efforts with numerical models, on the development of new cloud seeding techniques, and developing linked atmospheric/hydrological models to simulate the full hydrological cycle.
