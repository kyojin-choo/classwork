2. Gazdasag

[Pufajkas haromhatvanas kenyere: 103,60 Ft]

Megalapozott a pekek juliusi 10-15 szazalekos kenyeraremelesi
szandeka, a termelesi koltsegek ugyanis az utobbi honapokban
jocskan emelkedtek - jelentette ki tegnapi sajtotajekoztatojan Werli
Jozsef, a Sutoipari Egyesules igazgatoja. Novekedett az energia ara,
dragult a szallitas, a kenyerliszt. A tobb mint 3500 sutouzem bo
harmada ennek kovetkezteben csak nullszaldos vagy veszteseges
gazdalkodasra kepes. Werli kifejtette: a magyar fogyasztasi szokasok
elemzesekor kiderul, hogy egy ember naponta atlagosan 21,5
dekagramm kenyeret eszik, ami - szaz forinttal szamolva - 21,5
forint kiadast jelenthet. Ez kevesebb, mint egy gomboc fagylalt ara.
Tavaly egy atlagos magyar lakos 78 kilo kenyeret fogyasztott,
szemben az 1965-ben regisztralt 111,3 kiloval. A NSZ korkepebol
kiderul, vannak telepulesek, ahol a feherkenyer ara mar atlepte a
buvos szaz forintos hatart, mashol ennel joval olcsobban adjak. Tobb
helyutt - az emelkedo energia-, alapanyagarak, adok, beremelesek
miatt - elkerulhetetlennek tartjak a tovabbi, szeptemberre tervezett
dragulast. A kisebb peksegek altalaban ovatosabbak, feltik a
piacukat. A budapesti sutoipari vallalatok fontolgatjak ugyan araik
emeleset, de augusztus elsejeig meg biztosan a jelenlegi aron adjak
termekeiket. A VG szerint a Foldmuvelesugyi Miniszterium fontolora
vette, hogy a kenyer 12 szazalekos altalanos forgalmi adojanak
eltorleset javasolja majd a penzugyi tarcanak. Nevuk elhallgatasat
kero szakertok szerint az afa megszuntetese nem okozna megrendito
bevetelkiesest a koltsegvetesnek, mert a kereskedok termekeik nagy
reszet egyebkent is illegalisan ertekesitik. Az FM elkepzelesei
nincsenek osszefuggesben azokkal a hirekkel, amelyek szerint az
orszag tobb megyejeben legalabb 25 szazalekkal dragul a kenyer a
kozeljovoben.

[Csak eppen az alapproblemarol hallgatnak, mint suket diszno a
buzaban]

A kormanynak a lakossagi fogyasztas csokkentesere es az inflaciora
alapozott megoldasi kiserlete zsakutca - velekedett Szabo Ivan
partelnok az MDNP tegnapi sajtotajekoztatojan. Leszogezte:
alapvetoen harom teves kormanyzati lepesen alapul az orszag
valsagos gazdasagi helyzete. Az elso, hogy a kormany a szocialis
ellatorendszer radikalis leepitesevel egy idoben a realbereket is
csokkentette. A masodik hiba, hogy 1994 utan a gazdasagi
terapianak szamito belso fogyasztascsokkenteshez az inflacion kivul a
kormany semmilyen mas eszkozt nem vett igenybe. A harmadik,
hogy a koalicio nem teremtette meg a fizetesi merleg teljes
egyensulyat. A kormany konyvelestechnikai zsonglorkodesenek
nevezte Bogar Laszlo kepviselo a mult ev vegi 3-4 milliard dollaros
privatizacios bevetel stabilizacios szerepet, ugyanis a kiadasi oldalon
sehol sem tuntettek fel a nemzeti vagyon ugyanilyen osszegu
csokkeneset. Ma Magyarorszagon haromszorosan kizsakmanyolt az
allampolgar, mivel a realkeresetek csak 1,7-szer magasabbak, mint
1950-ben voltak, holott egy realkereso teljesitmenye ma ot es felszer
akkora, mint akkor volt. Rakso Gyorgy arra mutatott ra, hogy a
kormany nem vallalkozasbarat, amit jelez, hogy 1995 osze es 1996
tavasza kozott 40 ezerrel csokkent az egyeni vallalkozasok szama
(MH). [Mi az alapproblema: A MARIS TIZSZERESEN KIFIZETETT, AM
OROKKE KIFIZETHETETLEN ADOSSAG. Van egy csopp esze is Szabo
Ivannak, hogy ezt nem tudja? Az MDNP is nyakig benne van a
nemzet kifosztasabol valo reszesedesnek! Azert kapott 300 millio
forintot nemzetkozi toketol, hogy mindenrol beszeljen, csak a tobbi
berenccel egyutt a lenyeget segitsen elhallgatni! ]

3. Belfold

[Pufajkas es avosivadek egymast gyilkoljak az osszeomlott
Balkotmany romhalmaza alatt. Az orszag meg rohog rajtuk]

Az alkotmany-elokeszito bizottsag keddi, zart ulesen 5:1 aranyu
szavazassal, az FKGP elleneben ugy dontott, hogy atdolgozasra
visszakeri az Orszaggyulestol az alkotmany koncepciojat es a hozza
benyujtott modosito inditvanyokat. A koncepciorol szolo, mara
tervezett vegszavazas igy elmarad, az atdolgozott dokumentum csak
osszel kerulhet ujra a kepviselok ele. Tegnap delelott az MSZP-frakcio
elnoksege hasonlo javaslatot fogadott el, amelyet ma targyal a
kepviselocsoport. Lapinformaciok szerint az MSZP-frakcio ulesen a
vezetoseg foglalkozott Horn Gyula miniszterelnok-partelnok hetfon, a
tiszteletbeli konzulok budapesti tanacskozasan tett nyilatkozataval.
Horn az esemenyen kijelentette, hogy az MSZP nem ert egyet az
alkotmanymodositas tervezetenek tobb pontjaval. A frakcioelnokseg
tagjai kozul tobben kifogasoltak, hogy a kormanyfo a
kepviselocsoporttal nem egyeztetett allaspontot hangoztatott,
raadasul ez a velemeny elter a frakciotagok tobbsegenek
velemenyetol. Allitolag az is felvetodott, hogy a miniszterelnok a mai
frakcioulesen kerjen bocsanatot a mult csutortokon tortentek miatt.
A frakcioelnokseg egyes tagjainak velemenye szerint ugyanis a
kepviselok megalazo helyzetbe kerultek azaltal, hogy a partvezetes
es a szocialista miniszterek nem tamogattak az
alkotmanykoncepciohoz benyujtott modosito javaslatokat. Elhangzott
az is: farizeus a modositocsomagot elutasito szocialista kepviselok
ervelese, miszerint a szocialis jogok es az erdekegyeztetes hianya
miatt nem mondtak igent a javaslatra, hiszen ugyanazon a napon
megszavaztak a nyugdijkorhatar emeleset es a korhazi agyak
csokkenteset (MH, NSZ).

[Itt a kajan vigyor, megerdemeltek ezek hogy fejukre dolt a

Haz elott levo alaptorveny-koncepcio rosszabb, mint a jelenlegi
alkotmany. Vago Janos, az MSZOSZ alelnoke tegnap elmondta: a

Szakszervezetek Egyuttmukodesi Forumanak elnoke szerint a

alaptorvenyben. Palkovics Imre, a Munkastanacsok elnoke szerint az

Ha ez igy maradna, az "liberalis vadkapitalizmushoz" vezethet (MH).

onkenyuralom valodi neve: FASIZMUS]

[Melyul a koalicios valsag - akarmelyik pillanatban johet a krach]

Az MSZP elleneben az SZDSZ es az ellenzek szuksegesnek tartana a
hazszabaly-modositas visszakereset atdolgozasra, hogy a parlamenti
partok kozott konszenzus alakulhasson ki a napirend elotti vitak
rendjenek atalakitasarol, ettol azonban az MSZP elzarkozik - ez derult
ki tegnap az Orszaggyules alkotmanyugyi bizottsaganak ulesen. Hack
Peter, a bizottsag SZDSZ-es elnoke jelezte: tartozkodni fog a
szavazason. Az SZDSZ-frakcio ma foglal allast arrol, tamogatja-e a
hazszabaly-modositast. Egy kerdesre Hack hozzatette: bizott abban,

elerni. Szerinte az alkotmanykoncepciorol valo szavazas es a

vezetese nem tud kiszamithato allaspontot kepviselni, akkor az SZDSZ
is arra kenyszerul, hogy egy-egy kerdesben atgondolja allaspontjat.
Ez nem retorzio az SZDSZ reszerol - mondta Hack Peter. Toller Laszlo
(MSZP) kozolte: az MSZP-frakcio igennel szavaz a hazszabaly-
modositas mai vegszavazasan. Megjegyezte, hogy nem lenne
szerencses, ha az SZDSZ osszekotne egymassal az alkotmanykoncepcio,
illetve a hazszabaly modositasat (NSZ, MH).

YELTSIN AVOIDS JOURNALISTS AS HE CASTS HIS BALLOT... President
Boris Yeltsin voted near the Barvikha sanitorium where he is
recuperating from his illness and avoided the throngs of journalists
who were waiting for him at the polling place where he usually votes
in Moscow, Reuters reported on 3 July. No journalists were present
when Yeltsin voted, except an official photographer and a camera
crew from ORT. The Kremlin released a one-minute film clip showing
Yeltsin walking awkwardly, but speaking clearly as he exhorted all
Russians to vote. Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Moscow
Mayor Yurii Luzhkov reassured journalists that Yeltsin was fine,
ITAR-TASS reported. Communist challenger Gennadii Zyuganov voted
according to plan at his polling station in central Moscow, announcing
that "the West should not be afraid of me" and that Yeltsin's health
was "not good," Reuters reported. -- Robert Orttung

-AFTER WEEKEND IN ISOLATION. Yeltsin was isolated at the Barvikha
sanitarium under medical supervision with only his family present
over the weekend of 29-30 June, the Washington Post reported on 2
July. No presidential or campaign aides were present, suggesting that
the president was seriously ill. Yeltsin cancelled a meeting with the
presidents of Moldova and Ukraine scheduled for 1 July. The Russian
media have generally ignored the issue of Yeltsin's health, fearing it
will hurt his chances of reelection. On 2 July the Kremlin issued a
press release officially denying rumors that Yeltsin was dead. --
Robert Orttung

VOTING BEGINS IN RUSSIA... Polls opened early on 2 July in 2,200 of
Russia's 93,500 polling stations, in regions where voting would be
difficult to complete in one day. These included Chechnya and
geographically isolated regions in the north. Regular voting will take
place from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. on 3 July. Voting began in the
region of Chukotka in the Far East at 8:00 am local time on 3 July,
which was 1900 GMT on 2 July. By 10:00 GMT on 3 July, voting
returns from the Far Eastern regions showed turnout by midday was
some 5% to 6% below the level in the first round on 16 June. -- Peter
Rutland

-AS VOTERS ARE ENCOURAGED TO TURN OUT. The government and
Central Election Commission (TsIK) stepped up their efforts to ensure
a high turnout on 3 July. It is widely assumed that a good turnout
(above 65%) will favor President Yeltsin. Advertisements
encouraging voters to turn out continued to be shown on television.
On 1 July the government announced that all train travel would be
free on election day - to encourage people to travel back from their
cottages. ORT ran the climax episodes of Brazilian soap opera on the
morning of 3 July to encourage people to stay at home rather than go
to the country. Other vote-boosting measures included a TsIK
statement that voters would be allowed to use other forms of
identification besides passports, such as military IDs, and that extra
mobile voting boxes will be placed at stations and airports. AFP
reported on 2 July that some Moscow residents were getting phone
calls from TsIK workers asking if they intended to vote. -- Peter
Rutland

LEBED SEEKS MORE POWERS, HITS OUT AT ENERGY BARONS...
Presenting his national security concept to journalists on 2 July,
Security Council Secretary Aleksandr Lebed said he needs additional
powers to tackle Russia's immense security problems, Russian and
Western agencies reported. He outlined a wide range of issues
requiring attention, from the economy to the army to the
environment, and promised to oversee the implementation of
reforms. He was particularly critical of energy producers, accusing
them of plundering the country's resources, and called for state
regulation to ensure that they invest more of their profits in
modernizing the oil industry. In what appeared to be a dig at Prime
Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin, who has close ties with the oil and
gas industry, Lebed said the "energy barons" had acquired
"overwhelming influence" in several regions, the government, and
the president's entourage. -- Penny Morvant

-ADVOCATES TOUGH ANTICRIME MEASURES... In his national security
concept, Lebed also proposed a variety of measures for stepping up
the fight against crime and corruption. According to NTV on 2 July,
he said he intends to toughen the Criminal Code to introduce harsher
penalties for members of organized crime gangs, bribe-takers and
bribe-givers, and those who fail to observe contractual obligations, as
well as set up special courts to deal with particularly grave crimes.
The previous day, in an interview with Russian TV, Lebed spoke out
more forcefully. Asked how he intended to combat crime, Lebed
replied: "I will shoot them [criminals he cannot reason with],
reasonably, with minimum wastage for the police...those who cannot
be persuaded, I mean. In a civilized fashion." -- Penny Morvant

-AND REITERATES OPPOSITION TO FOREIGN SECTS. Lebed apologized
on 2 July for criticizing the Church of the Latter-Day Saints but
reiterated his opposition to foreign sects in Russia. He said he did not
want to offend anyone, but reiterated that sects such as the Japanese
Aum Shinrikyo are alien to Russia and have no place there, Russian
and Western agencies reported. He also qualified remarks he had
made about Russia's main religions, which caused a stir because he
had not included Judaism in his list. He said that the three he
mentioned (Orthodoxy, Islam, and Buddhism) were just examples,
adding "Judaism exists, just like Catholicism." -- Penny Morvant

BATURIN ON LEBED. In an interview with Moskovskie novosti (no.
26), former presidential national security adviser Yurii Baturin spoke
about his successor, Aleksandr Lebed. Asked to comment on Mikhail
Gorbachev's allegations that Lebed was "bought" by Yeltsin's
supporters before the election, Baturin rejected the idea that an
agreement had been concluded beforehand. He said, however, that
non-government structures close to the president's position did give
Lebed both financial aid and guidance organizing his campaign. --
Penny Morvant

DEFENSE MINISTRY PURGE IN OFFING? Rumors continue to circulate
in Moscow over the anti-corruption purge which Aleksandr Lebed is
thought to be planning for the defense ministry. Komsomolskaya
pravda reported on 2 July that many "enthusiasts" in the ministry,

CHECHEN PEACE TALKS IN JEOPARDY. At a meeting of the Chechen
defense council on the night of 1-2 July, military commanders
resolved not to attend the meeting with the Russian federal
delegation scheduled for 3 July to discuss implementation of the
peace agreement signed in Nazran on 10 June, Russian media
reported. Ekho Moskvy quoted Chechen Minister of Information
Movladi Udugov as stating that further such meetings were pointless
as the Russian side was "openly ignoring" the peace agreement. The
OSCE mission in Grozny called on both sides in the peace talks to
avoid actions or statements that could jeopardize the agreements
already signed, according to ITAR-TASS. Also on 2 July, pro-Moscow
Chechen head of state Doku Zavgaev flew to the town of Achkhoi-
Martan to cast his vote in the second round of the Russian
presidential election. Russian Nationalities Minister Vyacheslav
Mikhailov and the deputy head of the Russian state committee for a
settlement of the Chechen conflict, Sergei Stepashin, traveled to
Gudermes to assess the situation there. -- Liz Fuller

WORLD BANK LOAN TO BOOST KAZAKHSTAN'S OIL PRODUCTION. The
World Bank will offer a $109 million loan to Kazakhstan to aid the
rehabilitation of the Uzen oil field, the country's second largest after
Tengiz, AFP reported on 2 July. World Bank sources say that due to
financial and operating problems, crude oil output in Kazakhstan has
fallen to 17 million tons in 1995 from a peak of 25 million tons in
1991. The Bank predicts that by developing reserves oil production
could be doubled by the turn of the century. -- Bhavna Dave
