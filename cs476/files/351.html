[Kovetkezik a "Delvideki alapszerzodes"]

A szerbiai nemzetisegi kisebbsegek fenyegetve erzik magukat, s a
kisebbsegekhez tartozo, iskolazott fiatalok egyotode az orszagbol valo
elkoltozest tervezi - irta legujabb szamaban a Vreme cimu belgradi

Helsinki Bizottsag altal keszitett tanulmany megallapitja: a

kisebbsegi volta miatt. A tanulmany szerint a vajdasagi kisebsegek
az anyanyelvhasznalati jogok korlatozasan, a munkahelyi es iskolai
megkulonboztetesen, valamint a szerb nacionalizmus erosodesen
keresztul erzik jogaik csorbitasat. A megkerdezettek 39,7 szazaleka
panaszkodott arra, hogy a munkahelyeken eri megkulonboztetes, mig
az oktatas teruleten a valaszolok 28,6 szazaleka szamolt be
diszkriminaciorol.

CZECH MINISTERS DEAL WITH BANK FRAUD. Czech Justice Minister
Jan Kalvoda and Internal Affairs Minister Jan Ruml on 9 September
agreed to set up a special team to investigate bank fraud and other
large scale financial machinations, Czech media reported. The
creation of the team has been prompted by the recent collapse of
several Czech banks. Some 12 billion crowns ($440 million) were lost

apparently due to fraud. The special team will consist of the internal
affairs ministry's investigators, experts from the Finance Ministry

Czech officials have admitted that the country does not have enough
domestic experts to understand and deal with the complexities of
bank fraud. -- Jiri Pehe
