Menlevel a balkani nepirtoknak?

A vilagsajto naponta tolti tele az ujsagok hasabjait a volt Jugoszlavia
problemaival. Szorgalmazzak, megszallott modon kovetelik a "ket
fobunos": Karadzic und Mladic elleni bunteto eljaras elinditasat. Ennyi
nagy igyekezet lattan akaratlanul is felmerul a kerdes: ismet
zsakutcaba jutott a vilag kozvelemenye, sehol sem talalhatok elesen
lato politikusok, birok es ujsagirok?

Mindnyajunk szeme elott jatszodott le az utolso negy ev balkani
tragediaja. A vilag jo ideig olbetett kezzel szemlelte a szerbek
gyilkolasat es rombolo duhet. A haborut MILOSEVIC, a kommunista
szemlelet ma is hatalmat gyakorlo dinoszaurusza inditotta el.

Termeszetesen voltak buzgo hivei, akik megfelelo tisztsegek hatalmi
eszkozeivel hajtottak vegre pokoli muveleteiket a "divide et impera"
evszazadokon keresztul bevalt modszerevel.

Miert nem a fejjel kezdodik a leszamolas? Azt akarja a vilag s az
allamok elhivatott vezetoi, hogy az europai szellemmel szoges
ellentetben allo prepotens balkani gyilkos rendszer fennmaradjon es
megerosodjon? Az elmult negy ev tomeggyilkossagainak sorozata
nem az elso kiserlet volt egy fantazia-szulte politikai nagysag
problemainak megoldasahoz. A kereszteny vilagnezet visszautasitja a
Balkanon sulyos evszazadok ota uralkodo "a cel szentesiti az eszkozt"
elvet s a hazugsagon alapulo hatalmi oruletet.

Az etnikai tenyek megvaltoztatasa - barmilyen eszkozzel! - volt az
alapja annak a vilag altal szandekosan, vagy mulasztas miatt
tudomasul nem vett borzalmas genocidiumnak, amelyet l944 oktober
vegetol december vegeig jatszodott le a Magyarorszagtol elszakitott
Vajdasag teruleten. A vallalkozas sikere - az agyonhallhatas, a
buntetlenseg adta meg az alapot az utobbi evek borzalmainak
keresztulvitelehez.. Az egesz vilagon elismert "hos", egyes korlatolt
szocialistak szemeben "harmadik utat jaro TITO MARSHALL"- fenti
szellemu, irasos nyomok hagyasat gondosan elkerulo rendelkezese
alapjan tobb mint 40.000 magyart (gyermektol aggastyanig)
vegeztek ki a balkani moralban kondicionalt szerb "hazafiak"-
barmifele bun, va'd vagy akar letartoztatasi parancs nelkul. Az
aldozatokat kozmunkara hivtak be -nagyreszt a rendelkezesukre
alllo egyetlen fogattal es aso-lapattal felszerelve. A gyulekezes
helyen eloszor is kocsijaikat, allataikat koboztak el, azutan
megalaztak, megkinoztak oket. Akik mindezt kibirtak, azokat 30-
120-as csoportokban kihajtottak temetokbe, elhagyott utak, erdok
szelere. Itt megasattak veluk kozos sirjukat, a nagy hidegben
meztelenre kellett vetkozniok (oltozetukre ugyanis a kivegzok
tartottak igenyt), hogy egymassal osszedrotozva golyo altal talalva
belehulljanak a megasatott tomegsirba. A kivegzok parancsara a
magyar himnuszt es vidam magyar dalokat kellett enekelniok, mig a
halal orokre le nem zarta ajkukat. A balkani kegyetlenseg meg itt
sem ert veget. Nagy szamban voltak olyanok, akiket karoba huztak.
Ennek reszletes leirasa es olvasasa utan psychiatriai terapia lenne
szukseges. Ezeket a helyeket senki sem latogathatta meg -
ellentetben a mostani tragedia tomegsirjaival, melyek fölött a vilag
joggal gyaszban all, de azok - holt viragokkal ugyan - megis
kiviragoztak. Az ozvegyek, az arvak, a remeny nelkul maradt szulok
eszrevetlenul suhantak el a tömegsirok mellett, egy szal viragot ejtve
a fajdalomtol sajgo foldre, ahol legkedvesebbjeiket sejtettek.

"Marschall Tito", a tomeggyilkos pedig nem gyozott eleget tenni a
nagyvilag megtisztelo meghivasainak a Buckingham, az Elisee es mas
palotak szalonjaiba, ahol a vilag ismert politikusai lakajokkent
tolongtak korulotte.

Egyesek pedig expose-jukban peldakepnek allitottak "a nagy
allamferfit" a vilag ele.

Kerdes: Mi tartja vissza a vilag jogaszait, politikai szervezeteit a szerb
genocidiumok atfogo, gyokeres kivizsgalastol, s egy peldamutato, uj
korszakba vezeto biroi itelettol? Amig MILOSEVIC mint "grand
seigneur" fogadhatja az UNO, NATO, EU es egyeb szervek kepviseloit,
manipulacioival akadalyozva az igazsag kirajzolodasat, nem
beszelhetunk igazsagszolgaltatasrol, bekerol, csak intezmenyezett
bunreszessegrol.

Dr.Katharina Kajdi-Avar

"Blank check" for the criminals of the Balkan genocide ?

The world press fills columns of newspapers day by day with the
problems of the former Yugoslavia. Heads of the most zealous war
criminals Karadzic and Mladic must roll, they claim - so that justice is
done - by means of the International Court for Human Rights in the
Netherlands. Aren't there politicians or lawyers capable of
understanding the situation in entirety?

The tragedy of the Balkans in the past four years occurred right in
front of our eyes. The world witnessed, from a distance, countless
murders and the general craze of destruction by the Serbs. The war
was caused by the communist dinosaur MILOSEVIC. Of course, he
relied on many of his zealous supporters who carried out their
crimes according to the well proven principle of "divide and
conquer".

Why doesn't the account for these deeds start with the head? Does
the world and do the politicians want to sustain a system that is in
direct contradiction to basic values of western civilisation and human
rights, the murderous and prepotent system of Greater Serbia that
resorts to "ethnic cleansing" (a Serb euphemism? -synonymous to the
Nazi extermination of Jews, Gypsies and political opponents). The
genocides of the nineties are not the first manifestations of
megalomanic fantasies towards political and military omnipotence -
to "solve" problems. Christianity and Western civilisation contradict
this principle where "the end justifies the means".

The criminal and cruel activities of the past few years could only be
perpetrated because of the silent condonation and because no
counter-threats of effective punitive measures were ever made. The
criminals were thus reassured to have a blank check for their
activities! On this occasion it should be remembered that, in l944,
from the end of October to the end of December, 40,000 Hungarians
(including elderly and infants) were killed by the Serbs in the same
nationalistic fervour in Voivodina, a region populated mainly by
Hungarians and given to Serbia by the Treaty of Trianon in l920. In
order to leave no evidence or traces, no written orders were given.
The murders were effected following the orders of the "hero"
Marshal Tito, who according to some mentally limited socialists
represented the "third road"- whatever it might have meant? There
was no guilt established, no charges were made, no warrant of arrest
was issued. The victims were "called up to do some communal work",
(for l-2 days) and assembled equipped with their carts and spades.
At the collection points, the carts were immediately confiscated, then
the victims were humiliated and tortured. Those who survived these
crimes, were driven in groups of 30-l20 to cemeteries, some
concealed spots or to the edge of a forest with their showel or spade.
There, they had to dig their own mass graves and then strip naked in
the bitter cold - (the death squads wanted to make use of their
cloths). Afterwards they were tied together with wire, so that once
shot blocks of flesh would neatly fall into the already dug mass
graves. Before final shots silenced them for good, they were made to
sing the Hungarian anthem and happy Hungarian songs. But the
Balkan cruelity did not stop here. Many victims were executed by
the incredibly brutal medieval method of driving a pole through
their bodies (the mere description or reading of this horrendous
brutality would necessitate psychotherapy). Nobody was allowed to
see these places in contrast to the mass graves of the past few years
which the world rightfully mourns. The first graves however have
slowly become colourful -even if covered by wilted flowers. Widows,
orphans and parents left alone unconspicuously pass the spots they
know or deem to be their loved ones' last resting place and drop a
flower.

Marshal Tito, the mass murderer could hardly keep up with the
invitations he received from the world over, being officially
welcomed on State visits, courted and honoured by many politicians.
He was heralded as one of the great statesmen of the post-war era
up until his death.

The question that must be raised is this. What prevents lawyers,
judges and political organisations of the world from a radically
thorough investigation and an exemplary judgement that might
really lead into a new era?

As long as MILOSEVIC can receive as "grand seigneur" and "pacifier"
- the arsonist helping extinguish the fire! - representatives of UN,
NATO, EU and other international organisations and obstruct them in
their search of the truth through his manipulations, we can not talk
of justice and peace, but of institutionalised servilism.

Dr.Katharina Kajdi-Avar

Freibrief für die Anhänger der balkanischen Genozid-Politik.?

Die Weltpresse füllt die Kolumnen der Zeitungen täglich mit
Problemen des ehemaligen Jugoslawien. Man sucht, man fordert
besessen das Strafverfahren gegen die zwei
"Hauptkriegsverbrecher" Karadic und Mvladic. Solchen Eifer
erfahrend muss man fragen ,ob die allgemeine Meinung der Welt
nicht in eine Sackgasse geraten ist. Gibt es nirgends Politiker und
Juristen mit scharfem Blick, die über die Frage zu urteilen in der
Lage wären?

Die Tragödie der letzten 4 Jahre auf dem Balkan spielte sich vor
unseren eigenen Augen ab. Die Welt hat das Morden und die
Zerstörungswut der Serben lange Zeit unbeteiligt betrachtet. Der
Krieg wurde von Milosevic, dem Macht ausübenden Dinosaurier des
kommunistischen Geistes in Gang gesetzt. Selbstverständlich
verfügte er über eifrige Anhänger, die in entsprechenden Positionen
mit verschiedenen

Machtmitteln ihre infernale Aktionen aufgrund des seit
Jahrhunderten bewährten Grundsatzes:"Divide et impera"
durchführten.

Warum fängt man die Abrechnung nicht mit dem Kopf an ??? Will
die Welt und wollen die berufenen Führer der Staaten,dass das zu
dem europäischen Geist in scharfem Gegensatz stehende
mörderische präpotente System des Balkans aufrechterhalten und
verstärkt werde? Die Genozide der neunziger Jahre stellen nicht den
ersten "Lösungsversuch" von Problemen dar, die einem
gigantischen Grossmachtswahn und dem Wunsch.nach politisch-
militärischer Omnipotenz den Weg versperren. Die christliche
Weltan schauung widerspricht dem auf dem Balkan gültigen
Grundsatz "Der Zweck heiligt die Mittel" und sie widerspricht auch
auf der Lüge aufgebauten Machtbesessenheit.

Die verbrecherischen und grausamen Unternehmungen der letzten 4
Jahre konnten nur durchgeführt werden,weil allgemeines
öffentliches Totschweigen und das Fehlen effektiver
Strafmassnahmen das Tun der Verbrecher mit Erfolg belohnte. Es
sollte nicht vergessen werden, dass l944 (vom Ende Oktober bis Ende
Dezember) die balkanisch konditionierten serbischen "Patrioten"
mehr als 40 000 Ungarn (auch Kinder und Greise) auf dem von
Ungarn (in Trianon) weggerissenen Gebiet der Vojvodina
ermordeten. Hierbei wurden,um keine Spuren zu hinterlassen
keinerlei schriftliche Befehle erteilt. Das Morden war auf
Veranlassung des "Helden" Marschall Tito durchgeführt worden, der
nach Auffassung einiger borniert denkenden Sozialisten den "dritten
Weg" bestritt. Es gab keine Schuldigen, keine Anklage, keinen
Arrestationsbefehl. Die Opfer wurden angeblich für 1-2 Tage zum
"Gemeinde-Arbeitsdienst" einberufen -meistens mit dem einzigen
eigenen Gespann, Spaten und Schaufel. Auf den Sammelplätzen
wurden zuerst ihre Gespanne beschlagnahmt,danach wurden sie
demütigt und gefoltert. Diejenigen, die das alles überlebt hatten
,wurden in Gruppen von 30-l20 Personen nur mit ihren Spaten und
Schaufeln bewaffnet in Friedhöfe oder an Ränder von Wäldern oder
verlassenen Wegen getrieben. Dort mussten sie ihre Massengräber
selbst ausheben, sich in der Kälte nackt ausziehen,(die Mühe der
Todeskommandos musste rekompensiert werden ) ,dann liessen sie
sich mit Draht aneinander binden, um von Kugeln getroffen in die
fertigen Gräber zu fallen. Dabei mussten sie auf Kommando die
ungarische Hymne und heitere ungarische Lieder singen bis der Tod
ihre Lippen für ewig geschlossen hat. Die balkanische Grausamkeit
hat hier noch nicht aufgehört. Gross ist die Zahl derjenigen, die auf
Pfahl gezogen worden sind.(Dies zu beschreiben und zu lesen würde
nachher eine psychiatrische Terapie unvermeidlich machen.) Diese
Stellen durfte niemand besuchen im Gegensatz zu den Gräbern der
letzten 4 Jahre,bei denen die Welt mit Recht im Trauer steht.
Dennoch sind die ersten Gräber- wenn auch nur -durch tote Blumen
-langsam bunt geworden: Die Witwen und die Waisen, die ohne
Hoffnung hinterbliebenen Eltern huschen bei den Gräbern
unauffällig vorbei, lassen je eine Blume fallen auf die von Schmerz
brennende Erde- wo sie ihre Liebsten zu liegen ahnen oder wissen..
"Marshall Tito",der Massenmörder konnte den Einladungen der
grossen Welt kaum nachkommen in die Salons staatlicher Paläste,
wo die bekannten Politiker der Welt sich lakaienhaft um ihn
scharten.

Frage: was hält die Juristen, die politischen Organisationen der Welt
von einer umfassenden,radikalen Untersuchung und einem
beispielhaften, in eine neue Epoche führenden richterlichen Urteil
zurück??? Solange Milosevic als "grand seigneur" die Vertreter der
UNO, NATO, EUROPA und anderer Organisationen empfangen darf und
mit seinen Manipulationen die Wahrheitsfindung hindert, können
wir nicht über Gerechtigkeit und Frieden sprechen, sondern nur über
eine institutionalisierte Komplizenschaft.

Mivel a tobbsegi sajto rendkivul nagy gondot fordit annak jelzesere,
hogy egy lap kis peldanyszamu-e, ezt a gyakorlatot olykor nekunk is
kovetnunk kell. Es jelezni, hogy az a lap, amelyben Glatz Ferenc, a
Magyar Tudomanyos Akademia elnoke ellen igen sulyos tamadas
jelent meg, kis peldanyszamu. A havi Beszelorol van szo, amelyet
meg kevesebben olvasnak, mint hetente megjeleno elodjet. Az
eredeti es a mutacio kozott a folyoirat cime mellett a szerzogarda is
biztositja a szerves kapcsolodast. A lenyeg azonban a lap sulya,
hiszen a kisebbik koalicios part emberei irjak es olvassak.

Ugy gondoljuk, tekintettel Glatz Ferenc szemelyere, tisztsegere es az
ellene felhozott vad sulyara, hogy lapunkon keresztul biztositunk
nagyobb teret Mink Andras "Tenyarnyalo" cimen a Beszelo legujabb
szamaban megjelent irasanak.

Mivel a cikk targya kizarolag Glatz esszeje, a mottokent valasztott
vers nem ketseges, hogy kirol szol.

A szerzo Bozoki Andras es Kiraly Bela angol tanulmanykoteteben
(Torvenyes forradalom Magyarorszagon) Glatz Ferenc
"Tobbpartrendszer Magyarorszagon, 1989-94" cimen megjelent
tanulmanyat elemzi. A cikk alcimei: Szakmai hianyossagok; Valotlan
allitasok es A konceptualis alapvetes.

Az elemzes szerzoje igen rossz neven veszi Glatztol, hogy az SZDSZ-t
nem tartja jelzo nelkuli, hanem "radikalis liberalis" partnak. De annak
oka, hogy a cikket valojaban miert irta, ezutan kovetkezik. "Ezuttal a

voltak" - ismerteti Glatz cikkenek mondanivalojat Mink, majd atter a
kozvetlen idezetre - "a zsidosag kepviseloi" - "Majd egy reszuk",
folytatja az ismertetest Mink "es foleg gyermekeik - ahogy az elozo

hanytorgatta - a 7O-es es 8O-as evekre kiabrandultak az eszmebol."
Ezutan ismet kozvetlen idezet kovetkezik Glatzbol: "A kiabrandult
gyerekek kozul kezdetben sokan csapodtak a nyugati ujbalos
iranyzatokhoz is, majd beloluk lettek a leghangosabb
antikommunistak". "Glatz tovabb pontosit", veti kozbe Mink, majd
ujra Glatzot idezi: "Ezek az emberek tobbsegukben mar levetettek
zsido identitasukat. Ma mar nem is szarmazasuk miatt tartjak oket
zsidoknak, hanem az altaluk kepviselt hagyomanyos ertekek - azaz a
fejlett nemzetkozi kapcsolatrendszer, a felulrol bevezetett radikalis

Glatz szerint, idezi Mink, "-az SZDSZ meg tudta vedeni pozicioit a
bankszferaban es a tomegtajekoztatasban."

Mink ezutan igy foglalja ossze Glatz mondanivalojat: "-az elozo
kormany antiszemita es nyugatellenes propagandaja visszautott, es
nagyban elosegitette, hogy az SZDSZ szavazotabora gyarapodjon.
Ugyanis: "Ironikus modon sok magyar elhitte, hogy valoban letezik a
nemzetkozi zsidotoke, amivel a konzervativok a magyar tarsadalmat
riogatni probaltak, es abban remenykedett, hogy az SZDSZ kepes lesz
megnyerni a rokonszenvuket."

"A valasztasi gyozelem utan a szocialistak azonnal koaliciot ajanlottak

REGIONALIS HIREK

SLOVAK PRIME MINISTER ON RELATIONS WITH HUNGARY. Speaking
on Slovak Radio on 16 August, Vladimir Meciar commented on the
declaration issued at last month's ethnic Hungarian summit in
Budapest that called for autonomy for ethnic Hungarians in
neighboring countries. Meciar said the declaration is a continuance of
the "permanent effort, apparent in Slovakia since 1918, to renew
greater Hungary under various pretexts." He continued: "We
understand these autonomy [efforts] as the first step. . . . We have
the right to request that the Hungarian government publicly distance
itself from the signing of the document as an act that conflicts with
international law." The Slovak prime minister added, however, that
such a distancing is not a condition for a meeting with his Hungarian
counterpart, Gyula Horn. Meciar said his recently canceled meeting
with Horn had been postponed after "mutual agreement." -- Sharon
Fisher

SLOVAK POLITICAL ROUNDUP. Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar
attended the inauguration of the second of Slovakia's eight new
administrative regions in Nitra on 18 August, Slovak media reported.
Meciar stressed the importance of the new administrative plan, the
main goal of which is "decentralization and bringing the state
administration closer to the citizen." The Hungarian minority
accounts for more than 30% of the population in the Nitra region, and
the publicly elected mayors of three Hungarian-dominated districts
refused to attend the ceremony. Ethnic Hungarian politicians have
criticized the new system, as have opposition representatives, who
claim it strengthens the position of Meciar's Movement for a
Democratic Slovakia. -- Sharon Fisher

CARTER AWARDED PRIZE IN HUNGARY. Former U.S. President Jimmy
Carter on 17 August received Hungary's Order of Merit for
contributing to the development of bilateral ties and for returning St.
Stephen's crown in 1978, international media reported. The crown, a
Hungarian national symbol, was confiscated by the Nazis during
World War II and later captured by Americans. It remained in the
United States until Carter returned it to Hungary. Hungarian
President Arpad Goncz noted that the crown's return "fundamentally
changed bilateral relations and triggered the process of deep change
[in Hungary]." Last week, Carter and his wife, Rosalynn, helped build
10 new houses for the socially disadvantaged in the northern
Hungarian town of Vac under the auspices of U.S.-based Habitat for
Humanity. Carter and his wife were given the key to the city of
Budapest by Mayor Gabor Demszky, and Carter announced Habitat
for Humanity's plans to build houses in Budapest for Roma. -- Sharon
Fisher

U.S. WELCOMES ROMANIAN-HUNGARIAN BASIC TREATY. The U.S.
State Department on 15 August welcomed the successful negotiation
of a bilateral basic treaty between Romania and Hungary, RFE/RL
reported the following day. The statement described the agreement
as a "significant breakthrough" that demonstrates the two countries'
commitment to European integration. Meanwhile, politicians in
Hungary and Romania continued to react to the event. Hungarian
Foreign Minister Laszlo Kovacs on 16 August told representatives of
parliamentary parties that Budapest had "made no concessions" to
the Romanian side. A spokesman for Romanian President Ion Iliescu
on 17 August described as "an act of gross disinformation" a
statement released by Gheorghe Funar, the leader of the chauvinistic
Party of Romanian National Unity, warning of dangers posed by the
treaty. The extreme-nationalist Greater Romania Party called for the
postponement of the treaty's signing until after the November
presidential and parliamentary elections. -- Dan Ionescu

LEBED DENOUNCES INTERIOR MINISTER KULIKOV . . . At a 16 August
Moscow press conference following his return from Chechnya,
Security Council Secretary Aleksandr Lebed called on President
Yeltsin to dismiss Interior Minister Anatolii Kulikov for failing to
implement orders to normalize the situation in Chechnya, Russian
and Western agencies reported. Following up on his earlier promise
to name those "responsible" for the Chechen conflict, Lebed said
Kulikov has refused to cooperate with him, and accused the minister
of a "Napoleon complex." The Security Council secretary then issued a
de facto ultimatum to President Yeltsin, announcing: "you will have
to make a difficult choice. Only one must stay--Lebed or Kulikov."
Lebed bragged that the conflict could be ended in "20-25 minutes,"
but warned that if it is not resolved soon, Russia will face "a major
Caucasian war." -- Scott Parrish

-BUT KULIKOV STANDS FIRM. Kulikov quickly rejected Lebed's
charges as "slander and insults," adding that he would submit his
resignation and allow Yeltsin to decide if he should remain in office,
Russian and Western agencies reported on 16 August. ITAR- TASS
reported that on the evening of 16 August Yeltsin telephoned
Kulikov and asked him to stay on, dealing an apparent rebuff to
Lebed. Kulikov called a meeting of the Interior Ministry collegium on
17 August to discuss the Chechen situation, to which he invited the
Security Council secretary, but Lebed's press spokesman said his
attendance would be pointless, charging that the meeting would be a
"spectacle" devoted solely to finding ways to save Kulikov. After the
collegium meeting, Kulikov expressed regret that Lebed had not
attended but also criticized him, saying he "does not yet have a full
understanding" of Chechen affairs. -- Scott Parrish

KREMLIN DENIES THAT YELTSIN WILL HAVE HEART SURGERY
ABROAD. Presidential press secretary Sergei Yastrzhembskii denied
reports published in Time magazine that the president is planning to
fly to Switzerland for heart bypass surgery, ITAR-TASS reported on
19 August. The Time report claimed that Yeltsin had suffered a crisis
in June, during the presidential campaign, because he stopped taking

following Yeltsin's inauguration. -- Robert Orttung

GORBACHEV ON AUGUST 1991 COUP ATTEMPT. Important new
details about the August 1991 coup were revealed by former Soviet
President Mikhail Gorbachev in an interview with RFE/RL on 18
August--five years to the day since he was placed under house
arrest. Gorbachev said that on 30 July 1991 he held a meeting with
Kazakhstani President Nursultan Nazarbayev and Russian President
Boris Yeltsin, in which they agreed on the policies they would follow
after signing the new Union Treaty on 20 August. They planned to
remove the heads of the KGB and Defense Ministry, and to replace
Prime Minister Valentin Pavlov with Nazarbayev. Gorbachev said
that the KGB taped the meeting, and KGB chief Vladimir Kryuchkov
used the tape to persuade army leaders to support the coup.
Gorbachev also said that in June 1991 George Bush warned him that
a coup was imminent. -- Peter Rutland

BOMBS DISCOVERED IN PYATIGORSK, PENZA, KIZLYAR. A homemade

department store on 15 August, RTR and ORT reported. The bomb
was detected by a customer and was neutralized by police just 18
minutes before it was set to explode. Next day, another homemade
explosive was found near an apartment bloc in Penza, ORT reported.
Five rocket-launched bombs were found in the Terek River in the
Dagestani city of Kizlyar, ITAR-TASS reported on 16 August. Those
projectiles were most likely launched from neighboring Chechnya. --
